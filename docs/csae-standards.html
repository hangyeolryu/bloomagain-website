Safety Standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Last updated: 2025‑10‑30

1. Zero‑Tolerance Policy

Bloom Again strictly prohibits content, conduct, or services that involve child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including but not limited to: creation, possession, distribution, solicitation, grooming, sexualization of minors, or any attempt to facilitate such harm. We remove such content, permanently disable accounts, and cooperate with law enforcement.

2. Definitions

  • Child: Any person under the age of 18.
  • CSAM: Child sexual abuse material in any form (images, video, audio, text, AI‑generated or otherwise).
  • Grooming: Any behavior that builds trust with a minor to enable sexual exploitation.

3. Prohibited Content and Conduct

  • CSAM or sexualized depictions of minors, including AI‑generated or edited content.
  • Grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, or attempts to obtain sexual material from minors.
  • Links to, or instructions for accessing, trading, or producing CSAM.
  • Age‑misrepresentation to interact with minors for sexual purposes.

4. Detection and Moderation

  • We use a layered approach: user reports, automated signals, and human review.
  • We may use industry‑standard tools and hashes to detect known abusive material.
  • Content and accounts flagged for CSAE receive immediate, priority review.

5. Reporting and Escalation

  • In‑app reporting: Users can report CSAE content or behavior via the app’s report tools.
  • Direct email: abuse@bloomagain.app (or your designated address).
  • We promptly review reports and, where required, report to appropriate authorities (e.g., NCMEC or local equivalents).

6. Enforcement

  • Immediate removal of content violating these standards.
  • Permanent account bans for CSAE violations; devices and related accounts may be restricted.
  • Preservation of evidence and referrals to law enforcement as required by law.

7. Safeguards for Minors

  • Default safety settings for younger users where applicable.
  • Limitations on contact and discovery features designed to reduce risk to minors.
  • Education and guidance within the app on safe interactions and reporting.

8. Data Handling

  • We retain data associated with CSAE reports and enforcement in line with legal obligations.
  • Only authorized personnel may access such data for safety and compliance purposes.

9. Transparency and Updates

We periodically update these standards to reflect evolving best practices and legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For questions about these standards, contact: compliance@bloomagain.app
